Concept explanation strategy (how to write high-scoring answers)

For high-scoring long questions, combine concept + comparison + use-case + conclusion. Practice writing answers that show both depth and decision-making ability.

  1. Start with definition/context: write precise terminology in your own words.
  2. Explain mechanism: show sequence, logic, and relationship among parts.
  3. Add representation: use tables, bullet points, or diagrams where applicable.
  4. Apply to example: connect concept with a realistic academic or industry scenario.
  5. Close with conclusion: mention significance, limitations, or best-practice note.
