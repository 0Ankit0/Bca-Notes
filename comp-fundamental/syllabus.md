## 1. Course Description

This course is designed to conceptualize the computer fundamental knowledge and its application to solve real-life problems. It covers the concept of computer, computer organization, software, hardware, functional behavior of peripherals and important accessories, data communication, computer networks, and emerging technologies.

- **Unit I & II:** Fundamental concept of computer
- **Unit III & VII:** Software
- **Unit IV & V:** Hardware
- **Unit VI & VIII:** Data sharing tools and trends

The course includes theoretical and practical implementation of software tools and applications.

---

## 2. Course Objectives

- To familiarize students with fundamental knowledge about computer systems.
- To make students understand software, hardware, and their working procedures.
- To enhance students' knowledge about various components of computers.
- To train students about assembling/disassembling, general maintenance, and networking of computers.
